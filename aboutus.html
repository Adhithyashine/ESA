about us

Our products can be used for daily basis chores and can be used by the all types groceries shopkeepers, Supermarket, clothing stores, showrooms across all market places. They can be used in the households as well as hotels making it very convenient to use and handle your daily activities. Taking this step of Manufacturing and supplying paper bags,ecofriendly packing covers and reusable cloth bags, we have raised our hands against the plastic waste and took a step towards a clean, clear and a healthy environment and request and encourage you to do the same by using these 100% eco friendly products!!
